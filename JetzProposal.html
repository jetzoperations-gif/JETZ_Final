PROJECT PROPOSAL

Jetz Internal Operations & Audit System

Dear Sir,

We understand that manually tracking sales, ensuring accurate payroll commissions, and monitoring inventory is exhausting and prone to errors. We have designed a robust digital solution specifically tailored for Jetz Carwash and Auto Detailing.

The Goal: Stop "Service Downgrading" (e.g., charging for a sedan when washing a van), automate 35% commission calculations, and provide real-time operational visibility.

1. The System & Workflow (The Hybrid System)

We designed a flexible "Hybrid System" that combines operational efficiency with modern self-service convenience.

The Jetz Operations Portal - Secure Entry for all Staff Roles

WORKFLOW PROCESS

Physical Tokens
Greeter Scan

1. Hybrid Entry

Greeter issues a Numbered Token Card. Customer can optionally scan the QR on the card to open the Menu.

2. Operations

Washer performs the service. Status is tracked in real-time (Queued -> Working -> Ready).

3. Cafe/Inventory

Customer orders via phone by scanning the Token QR. Items are automatically billed to their Job Number.

4. Payment

Collection views "Job Summary", collects payment, and system automatically splits the 35% commission.

Real-Time Operations Monitor

The system provides a live "Airport-style" monitor so everyone knows the status of every car.

Figure 1: Live Status Board showing Queued, Working, and Ready vehicles.

2. Key System Features

Digitized Workflow Replaces paper logbooks with a secure cloud database. Every transaction is time-stamped and recorded.
Automated Commissions The system automatically calculates the 35% washer share for every wash. Payslips can be generated in one click.
Premium Digital Menu McDonald's-style ordering kiosk experience. visually stunning, mobile-first design with categorical navigation.
VIP Recognition New Feature: Auto-detects loyal customers based on visit frequency (Configurable Threshold). Alert staff immediately.
Smart Notifications Notify customers automatically when their car is "READY" via SMS/Messenger.

Premium Digital Experience

We have upgraded the ordering interface to a world-class "Kiosk Style" design. It features high-quality visuals, vibrant categories, and a seamless checkout process.

Figure 2: The new "Premium" Digital Menu with Category Pills and Visual Cards.

Collection & Notification Interface

The Collection station sees a complete breakdown of services and cafe items. The "Notify" button allows one-click customer updates.

Figure 3: Collection Payment Modal with 'Ready for Pickup' status and Notify options.

3. Owner's Control Center

As the owner, you have full control from your laptop or mobile phone:

  • Revenue Reports: View Daily, Weekly, and Monthly revenue charts.
  • Service Mix Analysis: See which packages (Sedan vs SUV) are most popular.
  • Inventory Ops: Track stock levels of cafe items and car care products.
  • Staff Management: Manage washer profiles and commission rates.
